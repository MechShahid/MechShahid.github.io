About

I am a computational modelling engineer with experience in heat transfer, CFD, thermo-fluid simulation, multiphysics modelling, compact thermal models, and solver verification and validation. My work combines first-principles analysis, high-fidelity simulation comparison, reduced-order modelling, and scientific programming to support engineering design decisions.

Featured Engineering Portfolio

Rapid Thermal Screening of 5G/6G Active Antenna Units

Developed a compact thermal resistance-network framework for front- and rear-side natural heat dissipation in active antenna units. The model used conduction, radiation, and natural-convection correlations and was validated against 3D CST simulations.

Forced-Convection Cooling Concept for Active Antenna Units

Built an analytical design framework for a front-side air-to-air heat-exchanger concept using fin count, channel dimensions, airflow rate, outlet temperature, and heat-rejection estimates.

Reduced-Order Thermal Modelling for RF Via Structures

Developed analytical reduced-order thermal models for localized electro-thermal heating in RF via structures and validated them against COMSOL finite-element simulations.

Sequential Electro-Thermal-Mechanical CST Workflow

Built and verified a CST workflow linking electromagnetic loss, thermal analysis, structural deformation, and RF sensitivity for thermally loaded RF hardware.

Verification and Validation of In-House Plasma-Flow Solver

Verified and validated the YAPS plasma-flow solver using benchmark cases including Poiseuille flow, de Laval nozzle flow, laminar jets, and free-burning arc simulations.

Unsteady Mixed-Convection CFD Around Oscillating Heated Cylinder

Adapted an in-house finite-difference Fortran solver for unsteady mixed-convection heat-transfer simulations around a torsionally oscillating heated circular cylinder.
